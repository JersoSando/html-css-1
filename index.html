The Green Bone Saga is the Greatest Series of 2021

What Is It About

The Green Bone Saga is an adult fantasy series that takes an urban fantasy and mixes it with conflicts of a mafia family. Jade is a substance that has the ability to grant people super human abilities. There's only two problems: not everyones can handle jade and jade is extremely rare and is primarily found in the small island of Kekon. There are two families in Kekon that control the Jade trade. They decide how much Jade goes out to other countries and who in Kekon gets to wear Jade. So what happens when there's a new drug that allows everyone to be able to get Jade and outside military begins to get too powerful?

Why Should You Read It

Do you enjoy

  • epic fights
  • intense and intricate political planning
  • flawed but relatablecharacters
  • crime family drama
  • expansive worlds
  • being so immersed in a book that you're at the edge of your seat everytime you open it
then what are you waiting for?! Each book in this trilogy is able to transport you into another world. With the epic battle scenes that will leave you wanting more and the conflicts between both prominent families fighting for power, there is never a dull moment in all of the books. You get to see the consequences of all actions and decisions made by these characters and how Jade leaves it's own legacy.

Final Thoughts

There has not been a fantasy series like the Green Bones in such a long time. Fonda Lee manages to create such an intricate world with complex characters that has you cheering, crying, laughing, and scared within every book. The timeline for the series allows readers to experience the conflict from the very beginning and grow with each character to the very end. She does not spare the readers feelings and is somehow able to make you feel like you are part of the family. If you haven't picked up this series yet- I highly reccommend you do.
